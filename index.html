Academic Profile

I am a Computer Science researcher specializing in secure, scalable, and resilient systems across IoT–Edge–Cloud environments, with a strong focus on secure AI-powered web applications. My research interests include cryptography and secure communication, containerization and Kubernetes orchestration, AI-driven deployment and automation models, and end-to-end IoT–Edge–Cloud architectures. I am the author of IEEE-indexed conference publications and a contributor of DOI-indexed research datasets hosted on IEEE DataPort. Alongside my research, I have substantial experience in undergraduate teaching and laboratory mentoring. I have served as an Assistant Teacher (BCA), Junior Research Fellow & Teaching–Lab Assistant, and currently as a Senior Research Fellow & Teaching–Lab Assistant at Visva-Bharati University. My teaching involvement spans both theoretical instruction and hands-on laboratory sessions in C, C++, Java, Python, Data Structures & Algorithms, Computer Networks, Full-Stack Web Development, Machine Learning, and other core Computer Science subjects. I am deeply engaged in bridging theoretical foundations with real-world systems implementation, and I actively mentor students in research-oriented thinking, practical computing skills, and scalable system design.
